Overview

This page organizes our weekly reading course (MATH-0293) for complex analysis.

The primary goal of the course is provide a standard, early graduate-level training in the study of a single complex variable. We hope to mirror the content of the "proper" complex analysis course MATH-0233, which has not been offered since Spring 2023.

The current meeting time is 12:30-2PM on Fridays. See schedule below.

Book

Primary text: W. Rudin, Real and Complex Analysis (PDF).

Additional useful texts may appear here throughout the semester.
