Research Interests

Convex Geometry

I am interested in many problems in convex geometry. As of now, I am most interested in looking at so-called "reverse isoperimetric inequalities" for convex bodies.

NEW!: I recently submitted a paper regarding a quasimetric that is defined in terms of mixed volumes. You can see it here!

Harmonic Analysis

I am also interested in harmonic analysis on Euclidean spaces. I hope to undertake more research in this area soon.
